FAQ

Common Questions.

Who is this program for? +
NextLevel Velo is built for high school and college-age baseball players — pitchers and position players — who want to add velocity, build functional strength, and train seriously. You should have basic gym experience and access to a barbell and some space to throw.
Do I need special equipment? +
You'll need access to a weight room with a barbell, dumbbells, and a pull-up bar. For the velocity work, you need a partner and some room to throw. No fancy machines required.
How many days per week does it require? +
The program is designed around 4–5 days per week — typically 3 lifting days and 2 throwing/velocity days. Each session runs 45–75 minutes.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. This is a no-contract monthly subscription. You can cancel at any time directly from your account and you won't be charged again.
What if I'm in-season? +
We offer an in-season maintenance track inside the program — shorter sessions designed to keep your strength and arm health without taxing your recovery during the season.
